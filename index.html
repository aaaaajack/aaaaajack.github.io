About

I got my PhD in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Eshan Chattopadhyay.

I am broadly interested in pseudorandomness, complexity theory and cryptography. My current research focus is on randomness extractors and derandomization of space-bounded computation.
